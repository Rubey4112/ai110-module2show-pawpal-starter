The four required classes are Owner, Pet, Task, Scheduler
Owner own Pet(s) with methods to list and add/remove pets and access their tasks.
Pet hold properties such as name, species and a list of Tasks with methods to add, list and modifiy said Tasks
Task hold a description, due date, recurrence frequency, completion status and methods such as mark complete. 
Scheduler class retrives and organize / manage tasks across pets to fit the owner schedule
